MISSION

To be able to provide a place with a laid-back atmosphere for Filipinos to spend their time for leisure, work or studies, along with a variety of good food and drinks, as well as games and movies for them to be able to unwind and relish the company of friends and family.

VISION

Elixir aims to be one of the top cafe chains in the country -- offering a warm and comfortable atmosphere for bonding, relaxing and studying, as well as a variety of quality food and drinks.
